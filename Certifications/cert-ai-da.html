💡 Study Tips

1
Start with AI-900 for Quick Win. Beginner-level, passable in 2-4 weeks, builds understanding of AI capabilities for data analysis. Getting certified early creates momentum for the harder PL-300.
2
Master DAX and Data Modeling. PL-300 heavily tests DAX formulas, data relationships, and star schema design. Spend dedicated time on calculated columns vs. measures, CALCULATE context transitions, and proper dimension/fact table modeling.
3
Build Real Dashboards. Create Power BI reports with real datasets, publish to Power BI Service, practice row-level security. Hands-on experience with workspaces, dataflows, and deployment pipelines is essential for both the exam and the job.

📅 4-Week Study Sprint

Week 1

AI-900 Foundations

  • Azure AI services overview
  • How AI enhances data analytics workflows

PL-300 Deep Dive

  • DAX calculations: measures vs. calculated columns
  • Data modeling and star schema design

  • Power Query transformations and M language
Week 4

PL-300 Practice

  • Mock exams and question review
  • Build complete dashboard project end-to-end
  • Workspace management and deployment pipelines
  • Row-level security and data governance
