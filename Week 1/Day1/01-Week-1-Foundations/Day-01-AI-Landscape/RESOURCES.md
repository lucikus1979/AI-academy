## Questions to Consider

As you explore these resources, think about:

1. **What can LLMs do well? What do they struggle with?**
2. **Why do LLMs hallucinate? Can it be prevented?**
3. **What makes an "agent" different from a "chatbot"?**
4. **What are the real risks of enterprise AI adoption?**
5. **How would you explain this to a non-technical executive?**

## Pro Tips

1. **Don't just read - experiment** 
   Try asking the same question to ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Notice differences.

2. **Take notes on surprises**
   What surprised you? What contradicted your assumptions?

3. **Connect to your work**
   As you learn, think: "How could this apply to my daily tasks?"

4. **Use AI Tutor**
   Discuss what you're reading with the AI Tutor. It will deepen understanding.
