## What to Expect

### AI Tutor Will:
- Ask you questions back
- Challenge your assumptions
- Push you to think about customer value
- Connect concepts to the real scenario

### AI Tutor Will NOT:
- Give you a ready-made presentation
- Tell you "the right answer"
- Write content for you

---

## Suggested Discussion Flow

**Minutes 1-5: Understand the problem**
- What's the real problem here?
- What does "AI transformation" mean to them?
- What are they actually afraid of?

**Minutes 5-10: Explore options**
- What could an "aha moment" look like?
- What would resonate with each stakeholder?
- What's a realistic quick win?

**Minutes 10-15: Synthesize**
- What's your core message?
- What would you definitely NOT say?
- What's your one key takeaway?
