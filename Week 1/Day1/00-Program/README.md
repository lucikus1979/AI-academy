## Daily Format

| Component | Duration | Description |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| **Live Session** | 90 minutes | Mentor-facilitated, situation-based learning |
| **Self-Study** | 90 minutes | Reading, exploration, assignment completion |
| **Total Daily Commitment** | 3 hours | Minimum required |

**Live Session Structure:**
- No lectures, no slides (mostly)
- Real situations, real decisions
- AI Tutor as your learning partner
- Mentor as facilitator, not lecturer

## Learning Philosophy: AI-Native

**The old way:** Instructor lectures → Student memorizes → Test recalls

**Our way:** Mentor designs situations → AI explains → Student creates

### What This Means for You

| Traditional | AI Academy |
|-------------|------------|
| Passive listening | Active exploration |
| Wait for answers | Discover through inquiry |
| Follow instructions | Make decisions |
| Individual work | Collaborative problem-solving |
| Theory first | Practice first |

---

## What We Evaluate

### ✅ We DO evaluate:
- Quality of questions you ask AI Tutor
- Artifacts you produce (code, documents, designs)
- Reflection and iteration
- Collaboration with peers
- Progress over time

### ❌ We DON'T evaluate:
- Memorization of facts
- Speed of completion
- "Correct" answers (there often isn't one)
- Attendance (though it matters)
