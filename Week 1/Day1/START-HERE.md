## How to Prepare for Success

### ✅ DO:
- Block 90 mins daily in your calendar for reflexion on AI Academy updates
- Observe, reflect everything with AI Mentor and reflect all new information by rephrasing all content in simple notes system
- Ask "why" more than "how", ask "what follows" to understand "why" better and ask ask AI to "share the context" instead of "explain"
- Embrace productive struggle - it's how you learn. The best way to learn is to get lost and get out by brainstorming with Tutor instead of following some guidance
- Build relationships with your cohort

### ❌ DON'T:
- Expect lectures with slides
- Wait for "the right answer"
- Work in isolation
- Skip daily self-study (it's not optional because real learning starts right here)
- Fear making mistakes - they're the best teachers
