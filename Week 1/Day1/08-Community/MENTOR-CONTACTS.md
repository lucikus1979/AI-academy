## How to Get Help

### Priority Order:

1. **AI Tutor** (for content/learning questions)
   - Open ChatGPT Enterprise → Kyndryl AI Academy - AI Tutor
   - Discuss your question or problem
   - AI Tutor is available 24/7

2. **Teams #ai-academy-help** (for technical issues)
   - Post your question with:
     - What you're trying to do
     - What error/problem you see
     - Screenshot if relevant
   - Tag @AI Academy Support for urgent issues

3. **Your Role Mentor** (for role-specific guidance)
   - During office hours: drop in
   - Outside office hours: Teams DM or email

4. **Program Lead** (for escalations)
   - Only if other channels haven't resolved the issue
   - Email lubor.fedak@kyndryl.com

## Tips for Getting Good Help

### DO:
- ✅ Describe what you're trying to accomplish
- ✅ Share what you've already tried
- ✅ Include screenshots or error messages
- ✅ Be specific about the tool/feature involved

### DON'T:
- ❌ Just say "it doesn't work"
- ❌ Send screenshots without context
- ❌ Email for urgent issues (use Teams)
- ❌ Skip AI Tutor for learning questions
