2 Governance Blueprint Overview

Why Governance Matters

This blueprint ensures agentic solutions are: explainable, governable, auditable, secure, and enterprise-ready.

The Hard Truths — Common Challenges with Agentic AI

Agentic AI has great potential, but effective risk management is crucial for trust, compliance, and business value. These four challenge areas are why governance exists — and why today matters.

⚙ Technical & Integration
  • Incorrect outputs / hallucinations
  • Legacy system integration challenges
  • Reliability & scalability issues
  • Vendor lock-in risks
🔒 Data, Privacy & Compliance
  • Data leakage & poor data quality
  • Regulatory breaches (e.g., GDPR)
  • Unintended data retention
  • Cross-border data sovereignty
👥 Ethical & Societal
  • Bias & discrimination in outputs
  • Lack of transparency in decisions
  • Autonomy creep beyond intended scope
  • Job displacement fears & change resistance
🛡 Governance, Control & Security
  • Weak oversight & monitoring
  • Unclear accountability for agent actions
  • Prompt injection & adversarial attacks
  • Identity spoofing & exploitation
⚠

Every card in the Governance Deep Dive (Section 4) maps to one or more of these challenges. The governance blueprint is not bureaucracy — it is the systematic answer to real production risks.

Documentation Best Practices

✅ DO

  • Write for the person at 3 AM
  • Include copy-paste commands
  • Version control everything
  • Update docs with code changes
  • Use diagrams over text
  • Name specific people, not teams
  • Include "what can go wrong"

❌ DON'T

  • Write "contact the team"
  • Use placeholder text
  • Assume reader knows context
  • Skip failure scenarios
  • Document in code comments only
  • Say "best effort" or "as needed"
  • Forget to update after changes
